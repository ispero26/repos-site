Why RepOS exists

A new rep should never cost you a surgeon.

When a rep leaves, their knowledge leaves too. The new rep walks in blind, faults the surgeon once — sometimes that's all it takes.

We've watched it happen. The company doesn't lose a case — it loses the surgeon, and hundreds of thousands to millions with them.

RepOS makes that knowledge transferable. The next rep walks in already knowing.

The app, as you scroll
00 · Sign in

Face ID and you're in.

Your book stays locked. Face ID in, auto-lock when idle.

01 · Your day

The day, on one screen.

Cases, lunches, coverage, credential clock. One tap deep.

02 · Your book

What you carry. Who you cover.

Lines by company. Facilities. One-tap backup — your book stays yours.

03 · Credentials

Never hunt for an expiration again.

Itemize once. RepOS tracks the soonest per platform and warns you 60 days out.

04 · Surgeon preference cards

Everything he wants, before he asks.

Per surgeon, per procedure. Recorded once. Recalled every case.

"I didn't even have to ask — my rep made sure it was on the table."

05 · Case planning

The whole case, before you walk in.

The plan builds itself from his card. Plan B, plan C, the failsafe — each with its checklist. Whatever happens, you have a plan.

06 · The construct map

The spine, level by level.

Plan the construct pre-op — screws, cages, per level. Tap any level to update it live during the case.

07 · Intra-op · live case

Tap when it happens. That's the whole job.

Tap-to-stamp times and counters. Durations compute themselves. No paper, no 8pm recall.

08 · The report

Already written by the time you're at the car.

Mirrors your Salesforce form, field for field, in its order. Most fields fill themselves. Copy, paste, done.

09 · Shareability

Knowledge that survives the roster.

Cards shared across the team. You choose what's visible. No rep walks in blind.

10 · Billing

The sheet leaves the room with you.

Captured in the case. Signed on-device. Sent with a tap.

11 · Ask AI

A thinking partner, on tap.

Fast answers, grounded in your own cards. Ideas the night before.

OR decisions belong to the rep and surgeon. AI is a resource — take it with a grain of salt.
